### Text Editor review

I'm a big vim user. A couple of vim tips:

- :s/[string-to-find]/[string-to-replace]/
- `/ search`
- D (delete current line)
- [number]dd (deletes number of lines above)
- 'b' goes to line beginning, 'e' to the end
- Cntrl + Z (save and quit at once)
